Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SPoT ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use the SPoT mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, phone number, email address, profile photo.
  • Property & Vehicle Data: Listings, images, and documents uploaded.
  • Location Information: Approximate or precise location (with your consent).
  • Usage Data: App interactions, crash logs, diagnostics, and analytics.
  • Communications: In-app messages and notifications.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and maintain core app functionality.
  • To verify users and prevent fraud or misuse.
  • To improve performance, stability, and user experience.
  • To communicate important updates and notifications.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. Data may be shared only with:

  • Trusted service providers (hosting, analytics, messaging).
  • Government or legal authorities when required by law.
  • Other users where interaction is an essential app feature.

5. Data Security

We apply reasonable technical and organizational security measures to protect your data. No digital system is completely secure.

6. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined, or as required by applicable law.

7. Your Rights

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent where applicable.
  • Request account deletion through app support.

8. Children’s Privacy

SPoT is not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of the App signifies acceptance of the updated policy.
