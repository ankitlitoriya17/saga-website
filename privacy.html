Full Privacy Policy

This full privacy policy provides the complete, human-readable text of how SPoT by Apna Sheher collects, uses, stores, and shares personal information. If you need a different format (plain text, large print, or another language), please contact privacy@yourdomain.com.

1. Introduction

SPoT ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect about you, why we collect it, how we use it, with whom we share it, and the choices you have regarding our use of your information.

2. Scope

This policy applies to all visitors to our website, users of our services and applications, customers, and other persons whose personal data we process in the context of providing our services.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information you provide

We collect information you provide directly to us, including when you register an account, subscribe to newsletters, fill out forms, contact support, or otherwise communicate with us. This may include name, email address, phone number, postal address, and any other information you choose to provide.

3.2 Usage and device information

We automatically collect certain information about your device and how you interact with our services. This includes IP address, device identifiers, browser and operating system type, pages visited, referring/exit pages, and search terms.

3.3 Cookies and similar technologies

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies for analytics, security, and to provide and personalize our services. You can control cookies through your browser settings; however, disabling certain cookies may affect site functionality.

3.4 Third-party sources

We may obtain information from third-party sources, such as analytics providers, advertising partners, or publicly available sources, and combine that with information we collect directly to improve our services.

4. How We Use Information

We use information for purposes including:

  • Providing, maintaining, and improving our services;
  • Personalizing content and recommendations;
  • Communicating with you about your account, updates, and promotional offers (where permitted);
  • Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, or security incidents;
  • Complying with legal obligations and enforcing our terms and policies.

5. Legal Basis for Processing (where applicable)

Where required by applicable law, we rely on one or more of the following legal grounds to process personal data: your consent, performance of a contract, legitimate interests (such as security and product improvement), and legal obligations.

6. Sharing and Disclosure

We may share personal information with:

  • Service providers and vendors who perform services on our behalf (hosting, analytics, payment processing);
  • Affiliates and business partners as necessary to deliver services;
  • Law enforcement, regulators, or other third parties when required by law or to protect rights, property, or safety;
  • Parties involved in a merger, sale, or reorganization of our business (with notice to affected users where required).

7. International Transfers

We may transfer personal data to countries other than the one in which you reside for storage or processing. When we do, we take steps to ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your information in accordance with this policy and applicable law.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as necessary to provide our services, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods vary by the type of data and the purpose of processing.

9. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect the personal information we process. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure; therefore, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Your Rights and Choices

Subject to applicable law, you may have rights over your personal data, including rights to access, correct, delete, restrict processing, object to processing, and portability. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@yourdomain.com. We will respond within the timeframe required by applicable law.

11. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to provide and analyze our services. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings and any opt-out mechanisms we provide. For more details, see the cookie section of this policy.

12. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent. If you believe we have collected such information, contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

13. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make material changes, we will provide notice through the website or other channels. The effective date is posted at the top of this policy.
